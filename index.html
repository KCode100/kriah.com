Meet Rabbi Zobin

Rabbi Zvi Zobin has been actively involved in chinuch for over fifty years.

He is the developer of the Zobin System, a scientific, diagnostic screening and remediation teaching system to rapidly remediate and upgrade reading skills in students of all ages.

For the past thirty years, he has worked with children with learning disabilities and has trained many people to use his system, which is now being implemented in countries such as the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Austria, and Belgium, as well as in Israel.

A prolific author, Rabbi Zobin has written many stories for children, newspaper articles, and books on chinuch; and run workshops on subjects such as parenting and learning Gemara with teenagers and has helped establish reading/learning services in schools and yeshivos.
